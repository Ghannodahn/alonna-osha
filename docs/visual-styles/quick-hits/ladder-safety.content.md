# LADDER SAFETY | 30 sec

## Checklist
□ Someone holds base
□ 3 points on ladder (2 hands + foot, or 2 feet + hand)
□ Belt buckle between rails (past rails? move ladder)
□ Top step = handhold only

## Quick Reference
| ALWAYS | WHY |
|--------|-----|
| Spotter | Base kicks out |
| 3 points | Stability |
| Stay centered | Overreach = fall |
| Skip top step | Nothing to grab |

## Key Fact
#1 injury. Under 10 feet. Not worth rushing.
