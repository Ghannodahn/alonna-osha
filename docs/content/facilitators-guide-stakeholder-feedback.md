## Reviewer Types & Instructions

### Type A: Safety Coordinator/Supervisor
**Who:** The person responsible for safety oversight at build sites
**Focus:** Regulatory accuracy, organizational alignment, risk coverage
**Time needed:** 20-30 minutes

### Type B: Experienced Volunteer Facilitators (2-3 people)
**Who:** Volunteers who have led safety briefings before
**Focus:** Practical usability, missing scenarios, flow effectiveness
**Time needed:** 15-20 minutes each

### Type C: New Volunteers (2-3 people)
**Who:** Volunteers with 0-2 builds who haven't led a briefing
**Focus:** Clarity, accessibility, confidence-building
**Time needed:** 15 minutes each
